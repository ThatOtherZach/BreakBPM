# BreakBPM

**The score tracker built for people who actually play fast.**

Most pool nights have a moment where nobody remembers who sank what, how long the game took, or who was genuinely on fire. BreakBPM fixes that.

## What it does

BreakBPM tracks every ball, every shot, and your live **Balls Per Minute**, a single number that tells you exactly how hot your streak is every game. The higher the BPM, the faster you're running the table.

BPM is calculated per-player, anchored at your first pocket of the game, so it reflects *your* pace, not a table-wide average. Every pocketing shot is stamped with your BPM at that moment so you can trace your pace shot by shot.

- **8-Ball**: Solids vs Stripes. Full rules including Golden Break and foul-on-8.
- **9-Ball**: Sink the 9 to win. That's it.
- **Practice**: Solo drills with unlimited racks. Watch your BPM improve session over session.
- **🦈 Shark Mode (1P 8-Ball)**: Solo 8-ball vs an invisible Shark opponent. See below.

## Why BPM?

Pool isn't just about who wins, it's about *how* you play. A 1.0 BPM game against a 3.0 BPM opponent tells a different story than the scoreboard alone. BreakBPM gives casual players a stat that actually means something at the table.

## Features at a glance

- Live per-player BPM + game timer
- HUD shows balls remaining for your group (including the 8), or "8-BALL TO WIN" once your group is cleared
- Per-shot BPM stamped in the shot log, trace your pace over the whole game
- Smart ball selector, shows balls you can legally sink and removes the ones you've already pocketed
- Full shot log with undo button (just in case)

- Works on any phone, no install required
- Free forever, but to save your stats you'll need an account ;)

## 🦈 Shark Mode

You're playing solo against an invisible opponent. Your first sink locks in your group (solids or stripes); the other goes to the Shark. Clear your group and sink the 8 to win, but every miss gives the Shark a chance to pocket a ball. It's an honor system, so you'll need to decide what ball could be sunk by a shark each time and then remove it from the table.

**Shark Mode Rules:**

- **Sink a ball** → it counts for you.
- **Safety** → never triggers a pocketed ball for the shark. It's a valid tactical play.
- **Only the 8 left + you miss or foul** → Shark wins outright.
- **Sink the 8 after clearing your group** → you beat the Shark.

**Shark Aggression:**

- **Normal** — Shark pockets only on a foul.
- **Hard** — Shark pockets on every miss *and* every foul.
