# Cancellation & Refund Policy

_Last updated: June 2, 2026_

This policy explains how to cancel a BreakBPM subscription and our general
approach to refunds. It is provided by Saym Services Inc. ("we", "us") as
general boilerplate and does not constitute legal advice. Where local consumer
law grants you stronger rights, those rights apply.

## How to cancel

- **Monthly / Yearly subscriptions.** Open your account page and choose
  **Cancel Subscription**. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current
  billing period. You will not be billed again, and your access continues until
  that period ends.
- **Day Pass.** A Day Pass cannot be cancelled; it simply expires 24 hours after
  purchase.
- **Lifetime.** A Lifetime purchase is not a subscription and has nothing to
  cancel.

## Refunds

- **Subscriptions.** When you cancel, you keep access through the end of the
  period you have already paid for; we do not provide partial-period refunds for
  the current period unless required by law.
- **Renewals.** If a renewal charge occurs and you contact us promptly, we may,
  at our discretion, refund a recent renewal that you did not intend to keep.
- **One-time purchases.** Day Pass and Lifetime purchases are generally
  non-refundable once access has been granted, except where required by law.
- **Statutory rights.** Nothing in this policy limits any non-waivable refund or
  cancellation rights you may have under applicable consumer-protection law.

## Requesting help

To request a refund or get help with a charge, contact Saym Services Inc.,
Vancouver, Canada. Please include the email associated with your account and the
approximate date of the charge.
