# Subscription & Billing Terms

_Last updated: June 2, 2026_

These terms describe how recurring subscriptions and one-time passes work for
BreakBPM, operated by Saym Services Inc. ("we", "us"). They are general
boilerplate and do not constitute legal advice.

## Plans

BreakBPM offers the following paid plans:

- **Day Pass** — a one-time purchase granting 24 hours of full access.
- **Monthly** — a recurring subscription billed every month.
- **Yearly** — a recurring subscription billed every year.
- **Lifetime** — a one-time purchase granting permanent full access.

Current pricing is shown in the app on the Passes screen and on the About page.
Prices may change; any change applies only to future billing periods, never
retroactively to a period you have already paid for.

## Recurring billing (Monthly & Yearly)

- **Auto-renewal.** Monthly and Yearly subscriptions automatically renew at the
  end of each billing period using your selected payment method, at the
  then-current price for that plan, until you cancel.
- **Billing date.** Your renewal date is set when you first subscribe and
  recurs on the same cadence (monthly or yearly) thereafter.
- **Cancel anytime.** You may cancel at any time from your account page.
  Cancelling stops future renewals. Your access continues until the end of the
  period you have already paid for, after which it ends.
- **No partial-period proration.** Cancelling does not retroactively refund the
  current period; you keep access until that period ends.

## One-time purchases (Day & Lifetime)

- **Day Pass** does not renew. Access ends automatically after 24 hours.
- **Lifetime** does not renew. It is a single purchase granting ongoing access.
- **Switching to Lifetime.** If you purchase Lifetime while you hold an active
  Monthly or Yearly subscription, that subscription stops renewing. You keep any
  remaining paid subscription time, and Lifetime access applies thereafter.

## Payment processing

Payments are handled by a third-party payment processor. We do not store full
card details. You are responsible for keeping your payment method current; a
failed renewal may result in loss of access until payment succeeds.

## Changes to these terms

We may update these terms from time to time. Material changes will be reflected
by the "last updated" date above and, where appropriate, surfaced in the app.
