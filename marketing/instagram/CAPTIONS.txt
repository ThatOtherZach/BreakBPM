BREAKBPM — INSTAGRAM POST KIT
=============================
12 ready-to-post graphics in the app's retro Win98 / CRT brand.
- 5 stylized squares (01-05) + 3 stylized stories (06-08)
- 3 "real UI" squares (09-11) + 1 "real UI" story (12) that show actual app screenshots
Square posts = 1080x1080 (feed). Story posts = 1080x1920 (stories/reels cover).
Link to put everywhere: https://breakbpm.com

HOW TO USE
- Feed: post the 8 squares as a carousel (recommended order below) OR space them out as single posts.
- A strong carousel mixes a hook + the real app: e.g. 01-hero → 09-app-hud → 02-what → 10-app-stats → 11-app-flow → 04-leaderboard.
- Stories: post the 4 story graphics with a Link sticker pointing to breakbpm.com.
- Pin "01-hero" as your first carousel slide / grid anchor.
- The "real UI" graphics (09-12) are great for "show, don't tell" posts and replies to "is this a real app?" comments.
